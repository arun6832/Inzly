# Day 11: Inzly Evolution — The Refinement Hub

Today marked a foundational shift for Inzly, transitioning from a discovery-only platform to a high-impact **Innovation Execution System**. We transformed the way ideas are processed, evolved, and built.

### 🏛️ Refinement Hub & Vertical Evolution
- **Version Timeline**: Redesigned the Idea Detail page into a vertical history track. Ideas now evolve through versions (v1, v2, v3), providing a transparent audit trail of a concept's growth.
- **Execution Status Layer**: Integrated lifecycle tracking for ideas. Concepts now progress through `Thinking`, `Refining`, `Building`, and `Launched` phases, providing clear execution signals to the community.
- **Asset Intelligence**: Added logic to show parent **Problem** linkage, ensuring every idea is anchored to a real-world challenge.

### 🤝 Collaboration & Team Forming
- **Approval-Based Flow**: Built a new collaboration system. Creators now receive "Join Requests" from interested contributors, moving beyond simple follows.
- **Architect Dashboard**: Implemented the "Team Management" component, allowing creators to view, approve, or reject pending operatives.
- **Automated Sync**: Developed a trigger that automatically initializes a private discussion thread and sends a "Welcome" message the moment a collaborator is approved.

### 🗺️ Map Intelligence 2.0
- **Hybrid Visualization**: Upgraded the map to handle the dual-entity model. Ideas and Problems now occupy the same map space with distinct visual identities.
- **Indigo vs. Purple**: Configured the map engine to render Ideas in Indigo and Problems in High-Severity Purple, with custom tooltips providing immediate context.
- **Entity Filtration**: Added a dedicated filtration layer to the map UI, allowing users to toggle between "Show Problems" and "Show Ideas" in real-time.

### 🛡️ System Stability & Hardening
- **Comprehensive Refactor**: Fixed numerous integration and TypeScript errors across the codebase.
- **Data Contract Hardening**: Updated global data interfaces to ensure full type-safety across the new hybrid innovation schema.
- **Build Verification**: Verified that the entire project passes full production build and type-check passes.
